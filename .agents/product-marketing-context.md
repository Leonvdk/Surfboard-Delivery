# Product Marketing Context — SurfRental Aljezur

## Company

**Name:** SurfRental Aljezur
**Domain:** surfrental-aljezur.com
**Email:** hello@surfrental-aljezur.com
**Location:** Aljezur, Costa Vicentina, Algarve, Portugal

## What We Do

Surfboard and wetsuit rental with free delivery and pickup to holiday accommodations (Airbnbs, villas, holiday homes) in the Aljezur area. We bring the gear to your door before you arrive and collect it before you leave.

## Service Area

- Aljezur town
- Arrifana
- Vale da Telha
- Monte Clérigo
- Broader Costa Vicentina (case by case)

## Target Audience

### Primary: Surf Tourists Visiting Aljezur (70%)
- European travelers (UK, Netherlands, Germany, France, Scandinavia) booking 1-2 week surf holidays
- Age 25-45, couples and small groups
- Intermediate to beginner surfers who don't want to haul boards on flights
- Booking Airbnbs/villas rather than surf camps
- Planning trips 1-4 months in advance

### Secondary: Families with Kids (20%)
- Parents introducing children to surfing
- Want safe, easy logistics
- Value convenience above all

### Tertiary: Extended Stay / Digital Nomads (10%)
- Remote workers spending 2-4 weeks
- Want flexible, affordable gear access
- May upgrade/swap boards during stay

## Equipment

- **Boards:** 6'6 shortboard, 7'0 funboard, 7'8 funboard, 8'6 longboard (all soft-top)
- **Wetsuits:** 3/2mm (summer), 4/3mm (shoulder/winter), 5/3mm (deep winter)
- **Extras:** Changing mat, roof rack pads, wax, leash (included)

## Key Differentiators

1. **Free delivery to your door** — no shop visits, no transport hassle
2. **Accommodation-based rental** — gear waiting when you arrive
3. **Local knowledge** — we surf these beaches daily and advise on conditions
4. **Quality soft-tops** — safe, durable, suitable for most levels
5. **No online booking system** — personal WhatsApp/email communication, confirmed within 24 hours

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Aljezur area)
- Local surf shops with rental (Arrifana Surf Shop, etc.)
- Surf schools that include board rental
- Other delivery-based rental services (few/none identified)

### Indirect Competitors
- Surf camps (include boards in package)
- Bringing own equipment
- Rental services in Peniche, Ericeira, Sagres

### Our Advantage
Most competitors require visiting a shop or signing up for lessons. We're the only pure delivery-rental focused on self-guided surfers staying in holiday accommodation.

## Brand Voice

- Honest, knowledgeable, unpretentious
- Like getting advice from a friend who lives there
- No hype, no "epic" or "stoked" clichés
- Practical first, inspirational second
- Local expertise without gatekeeping

## Business Goals for Content

1. **Rank for local surf rental keywords** — "surf rental Aljezur," "surfboard rental Arrifana," etc.
2. **Rank for trip-planning keywords** — capture surfers during planning phase (2-4 months before travel)
3. **Build topical authority** — become the go-to resource for surfing the Aljezur/Costa Vicentina area
4. **Get cited by AI search** — appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews for relevant queries
5. **Drive WhatsApp/email inquiries** — every piece of content should have a path to contact
