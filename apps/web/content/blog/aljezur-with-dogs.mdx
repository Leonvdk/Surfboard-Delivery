Bringing a dog to Aljezur works for trails and quiet-season beaches, but summer beach rules are strict and heat is serious. This guide is the friend version: what works, what fails, and when to leave the dog home with AC instead of a hot car.

## What are the beach rules for dogs?

Portugal restricts dogs on **many bathing beaches** during the **summer season** (typically **1 June–30 September** on supervised “concelho” beaches — always **read the local sign** at the access). Outside that window, **October–May**, dogs are often **allowed on more beaches**, still **on leash** where signed and with **cleanup** non-negotiable.

**Honest summer reality:** on popular **lifeguarded** beaches in peak season, you may be **asked to leave** with your dog. Some municipalities list **dog-friendly** stretches or **off-season** exceptions — **do not rely on forum posts**; check **current municipal** pages or ask your accommodation host the week you arrive.

**Heat:** sand midday burns paws. **Car safety** — never leave a dog in a closed vehicle; coastal parking lots hit **lethal** temperatures in minutes.

For **human** beach and surf logistics, [complete guide to surfing in Aljezur](/blog/complete-guide-surfing-aljezur) and [where to stay in Aljezur](/blog/where-to-stay-aljezur) help you pick a base with shade and parking.

## Which beaches are more realistic with a dog?

Naming “always OK” beaches ages badly because **bylaws change**. Instead, use this **decision tree**:

- **Shoulder and winter:** longer sandy stretches (**Monte Clérigo**, **Bordeira**, **Amoreira** at low tide margins) often work **early morning** if signs allow — **leash**, **distance** from bird flocks, **no** chasing wildlife.
- **Summer:** favour **non-bathing** rocky sections only if **explicitly permitted**, or **inland river walks** where dogs are tolerated — again, **signs first**.
- **Nature Park:** stay on **paths**; dogs can stress **ground-nesting birds** and **livestock**. [Rota Vicentina hiking](/blog/rota-vicentina-hiking) — same trails, **leash discipline**.

If your dog is **reactive**, skip **crowded** trailheads; west coast car parks get tense in August.

## Vets and emergencies

Aljezur has **small-animal vet** coverage for routine issues; for **serious trauma** or **overnight care**, you may need **Lagos** or **Portimão** (~**45 min+**). Before you travel, save:

- Your home **microchip** and **vaccination** PDFs
- **Portuguese emergency vet** number from your host
- A **muzzle** if your dog is anxious in pain (some clinics require it)

Tick prevention matters in **scrub** and **grassland** — ask your vet at home before the trip.

## Hiking with dogs: water, shade, and feet

- **Water:** carry **more** than you think; **no** stream drinking if you can avoid it — agricultural runoff happens.
- **Shade:** coastal scrub offers **patchy** cover; plan **early** starts in summer.
- **Booties** or **paw balm** help on **hot sand** and **sharp limestone**.
- **Salt and rinse:** post-beach **freshwater rinse** reduces skin irritation.

## Restaurants and patios

Aljezur’s dining scene is **not** big-city “dogs everywhere,” but **outdoor terraces** often accept **quiet, leashed** dogs — ask politely. [Best restaurants in Aljezur](/blog/best-restaurants-aljezur) is a starting list; **call ahead** in July–August if dining with a large dog.

## Accommodation

Self-catering **villas** and **rural** stays usually beat **hotels** for dogs. Confirm: **fenced** garden? **Cleaning fee**? **Bark sensitivity** with neighbours? If you surf, a **shaded** spot for the dog while you wax the board matters — plan **rotation** with a partner or **shorter** sessions.

## Surf trips and dogs

You cannot **tie a dog** on the cliff while you surf for two hours. **Options:** partner on the beach, **dog daycare** (research ahead — limited), or **surf** when someone stays home. If you are driving from elsewhere in Europe, **ID**, **microchip**, and **rabies vaccination** paperwork should match **EU pet travel** rules; Portuguese authorities can check at borders. ### Can I take my dog to Arrifana in August?

**Probably not** on the **main supervised** beach during **summer restrictions**. Look for **posted** exceptions or choose **off-season** travel.

### Are there dog beaches near Aljezur?

Municipalities sometimes designate **seasonal** dog areas — **verify locally**. Do not assume **all** west coast is dog-open.

### What if my dog hates heat?

**Skip July–August** or travel **only** for dawn/dusk walks. **Brachycephalic** breeds struggle disproportionately — be ruthless about safety.

### Do cafés allow dogs inside?

**Rare** due to **hygiene** rules; **terraces** are your friend.
