Amsterdam to Aljezur is a workable weekend or two-week trip via Faro: roughly three-hour direct flights plus ninety minutes' drive, often with same-day swell checks. Lisbon is the backup hub when fares suit better. Here is the no-hype logistics path for Dutch travellers.

## Flights: Amsterdam to Faro

**Transavia** and **easyJet** (and sometimes others in peak season) operate **Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) → Faro (FAO)** non-stop. Block **~3 hours** airborne; allow **2.5–3 hours** before departure at Schiphol in summer.

| Option | Flight time (approx.) | Typical use case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AMS → Faro direct | ~3 h | Fastest door-to-beach |
| AMS → Lisbon direct | ~3 h | City break + surf, or cheaper fare |
| AMS → Faro via Porto/Lisbon | 4–6+ h | Only if direct prices are silly |

**When to book:** School holidays and Easter move first. For July–August, start watching **2–4 months** ahead; last-minute deals exist but are unreliable. Shoulder seasons (April–May, September–October) often balance **price, crowds, and still-warm water** — see [best time to surf Aljezur](/blog/best-time-to-surf-aljezur).

## Amsterdam to Lisbon instead

**KLM**, **TAP**, **Transavia**, and **easyJet** fly **AMS → LIS** regularly. From Lisbon it is **~3 hours’ drive** to Aljezur (tolls on the A2, then N120 west). Choose Lisbon if:

- Faro direct is expensive on your dates.
- You want **2–3 nights in Lisbon** first.
- You prefer a **wider choice of flight times** in low season.

## Total travel time: door to accommodation

Rough realistic timeline **Schiphol → Aljezur** (direct Faro):

| Stage | Time |
| --- | --- |
| Schiphol arrival + check-in | 1.5–2.5 h |
| Flight AMS–FAO | ~3 h |
| Land, bags, car pickup | 1–1.5 h |
| Drive FAO → Aljezur | ~1.5 h |
| **Total** | **~8–10 h** |

Add buffer for delays. If you land late, consider **one night near Faro** or push through — the N120 is quieter after dark but less scenic.

## Car rental in Portugal

Pick up at **Faro** (or Lisbon if you flew there). Same advice we give everyone:

- Book **early** in summer.
- Request **Via Verde** or confirm electronic toll coverage for the **A22**.
- **Full-to-full** fuel.

You will use the car **daily** — beaches are not linked by useful public transport. For the “why,” [car rental for an Aljezur surf trip](/blog/car-rental-aljezur-surf) goes deeper.

## FAQ

### Is Faro or Lisbon better from Amsterdam?
Usually **Faro** for pure surf: shorter drive. **Lisbon** if the fare is better or you want time in the city first.

### How long is the flight Amsterdam to Faro?
About **3 hours** non-stop when operated direct.

### Do I need an International Driving Permit?
EU licences work as standard. UK and many non-EU licences are accepted for short visits; if your licence is not Latin alphabet, carry an **IDP** to be safe — check current Portuguese rules before travel.

### Can you deliver boards to my Airbnb?
Yes, if the address is in our **delivery area**. Send **dates, address, and package choice** — we confirm within **24 hours**.

### What wetsuit do I need in spring or autumn?
Typically **4/3mm** in **April–May** and **October**; **3/2mm** June–September; **5/3mm** November–March. Details in [wetsuit guide Portugal](/blog/wetsuit-guide-portugal).
