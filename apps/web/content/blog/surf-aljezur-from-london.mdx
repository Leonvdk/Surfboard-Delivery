London sends many visitors to Aljezur for good reason: direct Faro flights in about two and a half hours, then roughly ninety minutes by car to town or coast. Add a rental car and you can chase swell along the Costa Vicentina for a full week.

## Flights: London to Faro

Multiple airports in the London area fly **non-stop to Faro (FAO)** in season, often year-round on at least one route:

| Airline (examples) | From | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ryanair | Stansted, Luton, sometimes others | Often cheapest; watch baggage |
| easyJet | Gatwick, Luton, Bristol (regional) | Good frequency spring–autumn |
| British Airways | Gatwick / Heathrow (seasonal) | Sometimes pricier; includes hold bag on some fares |
| Jet2 | Regional bases | Strong in school holidays |

**Block 2h 30m** in the air; allow **3 hours** before departure at Gatwick/Stansted in July.

## London to Lisbon as Plan B

**easyJet**, **TAP**, **BA**, and others run **London → Lisbon** all year. If Faro is expensive, Lisbon can win — but budget **~3 hours’ drive** to Aljezur plus higher toll/time than from Faro. Worth it if you want **Lisbon nights** first or the fare difference is large.

## Car rental at Faro (or Lisbon)

Same playbook as every surf visitor:

- **Book early** for half-term and summer.
- **Via Verde** for **A22** electronic tolls.
- **Compact** is fine for two; longboards need roof carry or a big estate — we offer **roof rack pads on Premium**; say so when you [contact us](/contact).

Without a car, you are tied to **one beach** and infrequent buses via Lagos — fine for a chill week, frustrating if you want to match conditions. [Getting to Aljezur](/blog/getting-to-aljezur) spells out bus reality.

## Driving Faro Airport to Aljezur

**A22 west**, exit toward **Aljezur / N120**, then **north on the N120**. First-time drivers in Portugal: **keep right**, **speed cameras** exist on motorways, and **roundabouts** are common — yield to traffic already on the circle.

## Airport practicalities: bags, parking, and timing

**Bags:** Budget fares to Faro often exclude a **hold bag**. Surf boots, a second wetsuit, and a towel pile add up fast — check **both directions** before you assume the headline price. If you are not bringing a board, renting **board + wetsuit** here usually beats **airline sports fees** and the risk of a snapped stick on the carousel.

**Parking at London airports:** Pre-book **meet-and-greet** or **long-stay** for a week — drive-up rates hurt. **Gatwick** and **Stansted** have decent rail links if you prefer **train + hand luggage only**; add the **Overground/tube time** when comparing “cheap” flights from awkward airports.

**Timing:** **Half-term** and **Easter** fill Aljezur’s guesthouses early — align **car + accommodation + our gear** in one pass so you are not messaging us from a fully booked town. ## FAQ

### Which London airport is cheapest to Faro?
It changes weekly. Compare **Stansted, Luton, Gatwick** on the same dates and **include bags** — Ryanair/easyJet “headline” fares rarely tell the full story.

### How long from Faro to Aljezur?
About **1 hour 30 minutes** by car under normal conditions.

### Do I need a GB sticker or extra documents post-Brexit?
Check current **UK government travel to Portugal** guidance before you go — rules evolve. Carry **insurance proof** and your **licence**; an **IDP** is recommended for some licence types.
