Two weeks lets you stop pretending one beach is the whole country: common splits pair the south-west with the central coast, or stay south and mine Sagres and Lagos properly. This is not our single-place Aljezur itinerary—it is for multi-region trips.

A **two-week surf trip in Portugal** works best when you treat the middle of the holiday as a reset: one long drive day, then a slower second half. Surf fitness fades if you chase every pulse of swell; most people surf **four to six quality sessions per week** and use the rest for sleep, food, and driving. If the forecast flips mid-trip, having **two regions** booked means you can lean on Ericeira’s reefs when the southwest is blown, or stay south when the central coast is flat — flexibility beats a rigid day-by-day plan.

**Paperwork and peace of mind:** keep **insurance** (travel + any sports clause), a **photo of your passport**, and **emergency contacts** in your phone and a cloud note. EU roaming is simple for many visitors, but download **offline maps** for cliff roads. If you are hiring in one place and returning elsewhere, photograph the car at pickup — one-way contracts are stricter.

## Option A: Aljezur week + Ericeira week

**Why it works:** You get **natural park vibes**, **hiking**, and **beach variety** first — then **surf-town energy**, **more reefs/points**, and **easier Lisbon airport** for the return.

### Week 1 — Base: Aljezur (Costa Vicentina)

| Day | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Arrive **Faro**, drive ~1.5h, settle, scout **Arrifana** |
| 2–3 | Surf **Arrifana** / **Monte Clérigo** — read [Arrifana guide](/blog/arrifana-surf-guide) |
| 4 | **Amoreira** or **Vale Figueiras** if swell suits |
| 5 | Rest / **Rota Vicentina** hike |
| 6 | **Sagres day** (~40 min) — lighthouse, different exposures |
| 7 | Surf favourite + pack for north |

**Tip:** Don't burn yourself out — **two sessions max** on heavy days; [surf safety](/blog/surf-safety-portugal) matters on a long trip.

### Relocation day

Drive **Aljezur → Ericeira** in roughly **3 hours** without drama stops. Fuel and tolls add up — budget **€40–60** for the leg including snacks.

### Week 2 — Base: Ericeira area

| Day | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 8 | Arrive midday, afternoon easy surf at a **mild** beach |
| 9–11 | Sample **2–3 different peaks** — respect local crowds |
| 12 | Rest or **Lisbon** culture hit (45–60 min) |
| 13 | Best forecast day — go early |
| 14 | Final morning optional splash, **Lisbon** fly out |

**Alternative second week:** Swap Ericeira for **Peniche/Baleal** if you want **sandbars** and **lesson-friendly** setups more than cobblestone town charm.

## Option B: All south — Aljezur + Lagos + Sagres loops

**Why it works:** **Zero long northbound drives**; you stay **Faro in / Faro out**; you deepen **southwest knowledge** instead of rushing.

| Block | Nights (suggested) | Highlights |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Aljezur core | 8–9 | Daily surf + [one-week style rhythm](/blog/one-week-aljezur-itinerary) |
| Lagos / west Algarve | 3–4 | Different bays, boat culture, restaurants |
| Sagres pocket | 2–3 | Raw headlands, Tonel/Beliche on right swell |

You'll still drive plenty — but each leg is **under an hour** rather than **cross-country**.

## Logistics that make or break the trip

**One-way car hire** (Faro → Lisbon or reverse) can carry **surcharges** — compare **return same airport** vs **one-way total**.

**Board transport:** renting **soft-tops** locally avoids airline fees. We serve **Aljezur area** with **delivery** — for the **central coast leg**, arrange **another rental there** or bring your own. Our [board guide](/blog/what-surfboard-should-i-rent) helps you pick sizes before you message.

**Accommodation:** book **two separate stays** with **one clear moving day** — mid-trip apartment swaps are easier than nightly hops.

## When to do this trip

**May–June** and **September–October** balance **swell**, **warmth**, and **parking sanity**. **August** works but **costs more** and **crowds** spike — [best time Aljezur](/blog/best-time-to-surf-aljezur) is a good southern-coast anchor.

### FAQ

### Is two weeks too long for beginners?

Not if you **pace sessions** and take **rest days**. Mix **lessons** early week, independence later.

### Faro or Lisbon airport?

**Faro** for Option B (all south). **Option A** can be **Faro in, Lisbon out** — compare one-way fees.

### Can I surf every day for fourteen days?

Your shoulders will vote **no**. Plan **2–3 rest days** — hike, sleep, eat.

### Is this better than two weeks only in Aljezur?

**Different goal** — one region **deep** vs **country sampler**. Both are valid; we wrote the **single-base** version [here](/blog/two-week-aljezur-itinerary).
