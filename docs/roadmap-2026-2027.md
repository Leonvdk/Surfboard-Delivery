## Recurring rituals (not dated but non-negotiable)

- **Weekly Sunday review** (15 min): GBP photos, reviews, bookings vs same-week-last-year.
- **Weekly Monday GBP routine** (30 min): 1 photo + 1 post + reply to all reviews (SUR-15).
- **Bi-weekly GSC near-miss export** (30 min): copy 5 near-miss queries to `docs/gsc-near-misses.md` (SUR-24).
- **Monthly first-Sunday** (45 min): walk this roadmap top-to-bottom + `docs/strategic-plan-2026-27.md`.
- **Quarterly first-Sunday** (90 min): rewrite the current phase, refresh top-10 posts, full schema audit.

## Success KPIs (measured monthly)

- Google reviews: **8 → 15** (Oct) → **30** (Feb) → **50** (Sep 2027).
- Blog posts published or refreshed: **1/week** through Nov, **1/month** Dec-Feb (freeze), **1/week** Mar-Sep.
- Backlinks landed: **3+** (Nov sprint), **10+** total (by Sep 2027).
- Portugal citations submitted: **10** (Aug) → **15** (Nov audit).
- Organic sessions: baseline in Aug → **3-5×** by Jul 2027 (weighted toward Jan-Aug).
- First-party data posts: **5** across the year (Aug, Sep, Oct, Dec, May) minimum.

## When to break glass on this roadmap

Trigger events that mean stop-and-replan:

- **Sep 2026 comes in flat** (<80% of 2025) → tighten Q4 content pipeline, add 2 posts.
- **GBP not verified by 15 Aug** → escalate: reorder postcard, try video verification.
- **Jan-Feb 2027 traffic under-performs** → 2-week emergency SEO audit; reset Mar-Jun plan.
- **A blog breaks into top-3 for its target query** → double down on that topic cluster (add 3-4 sibling posts).
- **Any month with 20+ new bookings** → refresh first-party data posts with new numbers.
